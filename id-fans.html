ID Fans

High-Efficiency Industrial Ventilation Solution

An induced draft fan is an important mechanical device that effectively extracts air or combustion gases from boilers, furnaces, and incinerators by creating negative pressure in a system. The induced draft fans are used in many industrial applications where the removal of gases and control of ventilation are of utmost importance. Induced draft fans are necessary industrial fans because they are designed to handle large air or gas volumes while maintaining the ideal pressure conditions, and, as a respectable ID Fan Manufacturer in India, we guarantee the precision of our systems to meet the demanding specifications of many different industries.

Product Description

Through the inlet, ID fans draw gases or air into the fan casing. The revolving impeller blades speed up the gases as they enter the casing and exit the outlet at a faster rate. Being a reputable ID fan supplier in India, we can guarantee that every ID fan is planned, constructed, and tested for peak performance, demonstrating high efficiency and a steady airflow rate to meet the needs of various industrial applications.

Usually placed between the dust collector and the chimney or stack, ID fans are situated downstream of the primary process equipment. Because of their location, they can handle cleaned gases following particulate removal, which lowers wear and prolongs the life of the equipment.
