Our Story

We at R.K. Industries began our journey humbly in the year 2005 and became a popular name among the industrialists retailers & other individuals due to our excellent quality of work. As a manufacturer we have been devoted to provide premium quality of industrial products to customers all across India. Our professional punctual and quality centric work approaches helped us in earning trust of customers. And becoming the most valuable supplier in the domain. We have been responsibly fulfilling the growing requirements of customers for products such as Industrial Air Washer Draught Fan Parts Axial Flow Fan Industrial Aluminum Louvers Temperature Burner Hot Air Systems and others.
