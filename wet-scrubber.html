Applications

A wet scrubber uses the liquid to interact with the contaminated gas so that dust, fumes, and hazardous gases can be absorbed and eliminated. Once the process is done, the clean air is discharged from the scrubber and the liquid that remains contains the contaminants, which can either be treated or disposed of.
